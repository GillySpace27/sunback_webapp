Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 2026-05-28

Our commitment

My Heliograph (operated by Chris Gilly) is committed to ensuring My Heliograph is accessible to as many people as possible, including people who use assistive technologies. We aim for conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA.

This statement is published in connection with our obligations under the European Accessibility Act (Directive (EU) 2019/882), which came into force on 28 June 2025.

Conformance status

The site is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. The known non-conformances are listed below; we are actively closing them.

What works

  • Keyboard navigation through all primary workflows (product picker → image picker → editor) without a mouse
  • Visible focus indicators on interactive elements (commit 0a98244 introduced a global focus-visible ring)
  • Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 for body text and ≥ 3:1 for non-text UI in the default dark theme
  • Proper landmark structure (<main>, <nav>, <footer>)
  • Step badges and section headings convey the user's position in the workflow
  • Modals trap focus and close on Escape
  • prefers-reduced-motion respected: radial wipes, fades, and scroll animations short-circuit when the OS preference is set
  • Solar-image content carries descriptive alt attributes
  • Cookie banner does not block content and can be operated by keyboard

Known gaps

  • Some form controls (<input type="date">, master toggle radio buttons inside the variant modal) rely on visible-text labels rather than programmatic <label> association — being addressed in the current sprint.
  • The radial-wipe animation on the master toggle can be visually intense; it is short-circuited under prefers-reduced-motion but a hard-stop “disable all motion” toggle is on the roadmap.
  • The product-mockup canvas does not currently announce its content to screen readers. We surface the product name in a centred heading above the canvas as a workaround.
  • The custom HEK event-picker tiles have basic ARIA labelling but the keyboard focus order across the 4-tile grid is left-to-right + top-to-bottom only (no arrow-key navigation).

Preparation of this statement

This statement was prepared on 2026-05-28. The audit was self-assessment using automated tooling (Lighthouse, axe-core) and manual keyboard + screen-reader walk-throughs (VoiceOver on macOS, NVDA on Windows). A third-party audit is planned for Q1 2027.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on My Heliograph, please contact us at gillygumption@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days and to remediate identified issues within 30 days where feasible.

If you are not satisfied with our response, EU residents may escalate to their national supervisory body for the European Accessibility Act. UK residents may contact the Equality and Human Rights Commission. US residents may contact the Department of Justice ADA Information Line.

Enforcement procedure

If we cannot resolve your concern, you can lodge a complaint with the appropriate enforcement body in your jurisdiction. We will assist you with the contact details on request.
